Common questions

I found a bug in one of the tools. What should I do?
Email us with the tool name, what you were trying to do, what happened instead, and which browser and device you were using. Screenshots are always helpful.
Can you build a custom tool for my business?
Yes — we build custom browser-based tools and websites. Get in touch via email with a description of what you need and we'll discuss it.
I have an idea for a new tool. Will you build it?
We love tool suggestions — send it over and if it fits the Neatkit philosophy (free, browser-based, no signup) we'll add it to the roadmap.
How long does it take to get a response?
Usually within 24–48 hours on weekdays. We're a small team so we appreciate your patience during busy periods.
