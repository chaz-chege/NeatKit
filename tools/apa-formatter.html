Key APA 7th Edition rules
✍️
Author format
Last name, First initial. Middle initial. — e.g., Okonkwo, C. J. Up to 20 authors listed; 21+ use ellipsis then last author.
📅
Year in parentheses
Always place year in parentheses immediately after authors: Okonkwo, C. J. (2023).
🔤
Title capitalization
Article and chapter titles: only first word, first word after colon, and proper nouns. Book titles: same rule, then italicize.
📐
Hanging indent
First line of each reference is flush left; subsequent lines indented 0.5 inches (1.27cm). References are alphabetized by author surname.
🔗
DOI format
Use hyperlink format: https://doi.org/xxxxx — No "Retrieved from" needed for DOIs. URL retrieval dates only needed when content may change.
📖
In-text citations
Parenthetical: (Author, Year) or (Author, Year, p. X) for quotes. Narrative: Author (Year) said... — For 3+ authors use et al. from first citation.
🌐
No publisher location
APA 7th removed publisher location for books. Just: Publisher Name. Also removed "Retrieved from" for most stable URLs.
