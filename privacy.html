Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Short version: Neatkit does not collect, store, or transmit your files or documents. All file processing happens locally in your browser. We use Google AdSense to display ads, which may use cookies. That's it.

1. Who we are

Neatkit ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at neatkit.com. We provide free online productivity tools including image compression, PDF utilities, text analysis tools, and unit converters.

2. What data we collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • Files you upload or process — all processing happens in your browser using JavaScript. Your files, images, PDFs, and text are never sent to our servers. We have no access to them whatsoever.
  • Text you paste into tools — processed entirely in your browser. Never transmitted or stored.
  • Usage data — we may use standard web analytics (such as page view counts) that do not identify individual users.

3. Cookies and advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history across websites. This is standard practice for ad-supported free services.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, visit: How Google uses data from partner sites.

4. Third-party libraries

Some tools load third-party JavaScript libraries (such as PDF.js for PDF processing, or pdf-lib for PDF creation) from content delivery networks. These are loaded solely to power tool functionality and do not collect your data.

5. Children's privacy

Neatkit is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Your rights

Since we do not store personal data, there is nothing to request deletion of. If you have concerns about advertising data collected by Google, you can manage your preferences at Google's Privacy Controls.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our service evolves. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of Neatkit after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at: privacy@neatkit.com

This privacy policy was written to comply with Google AdSense requirements and general best practices for browser-based tools.
